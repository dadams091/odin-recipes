Stir Fry

Description

Start by heating a tablespoon of oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add sliced vegetables like bell peppers, onions, broccoli, and carrots. Stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes until the veggies are tender-crisp. Add in your choice of protein, such as chicken, beef, or tofu, and cook until fully browned and cooked through.

For the sauce, mix together soy sauce, garlic, ginger, a bit of honey or sugar, and a splash of vinegar or lime juice. Pour the sauce over the cooked ingredients in the pan and toss everything to coat. Let it cook for another 2-3 minutes until everything is well combined and the sauce thickens. Serve your stir-fry with steamed rice or noodles for a quick and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 pound protein (chicken, beef, or tofu)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar or lime juice
  • Steamed rice or noodles (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add sliced bell pepper, onion, broccoli, and carrot. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  3. Add protein (chicken, beef, or tofu) and cook until fully browned and cooked through.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together 3 tablespoons of soy sauce, 2 cloves of minced garlic, 1 tablespoon grated ginger, 1 tablespoon honey or sugar, and 1 tablespoon vinegar or lime juice.
  5. Pour the sauce over the cooked ingredients and toss to coat. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Serve with steamed rice or noodles.
